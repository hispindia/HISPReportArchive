 Contacts registered for cases  

A person having close proximity to a leprosy patient for a prolonged duration. Such persons are considered “exposed” to leprosy and may or may not have been infected. “Prolonged duration” is typically defined as having been in contact with an untreated patient for 20 hours per week for at least three months in a year, e.g. family members, neighbours, friends, school children in same class; co-workers in same office, etc.
